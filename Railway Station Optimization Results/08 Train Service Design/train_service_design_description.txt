TITLE
  Scenario 2 — OD-Locked 3-Train Service Corridor Design
  for the Optimized 15-Station Network

OVERVIEW
  OD-locked train service route design for three train
  services operating on the Scenario 2 optimized 15-station
  network. Each train is assigned a fixed origin-destination
  pair with an explicit ordered station sequence.

HOW THEY WERE GENERATED
  Station paths were designed manually based on the MST
  topology of the 15 selected stations, grouping geographically
  contiguous stations into logical service corridors. The
  "OD-locked" design means paths are fixed and do not use
  inferred or interpolated routing — every stop is explicitly
  specified. The forward_path field gives the ordered sequence
  of stations from origin to destination.

PURPOSE
  Provides an operational service pattern for the recommended
  15-station network, demonstrating feasibility of serving
  the selected stations with 3 train services. Used to
  generate the service corridor map visualization (OD.py).

VIEWING ORDER
  Dataset #8 — final dataset in the sequence. Review after
  understanding the Scenario 2 station selection (Group 07).

TRAIN SERVICE PATTERNS:
  Train 1 : Umuahia → Enugu → Abuja → Birnin Kebbi
  Train 2 : Chigo → Abuja → Funtua → Dutse → Damaturu → Hausari
  Train 3 : Abeokuta → Oshogbo → Boji-Boji → Enugu → Abuja → Funtua → Bauchi → Gombe → Yola

SCHEMA — train_service_design.json
  Per train: origin, destination, forward_path (ordered list)

SCHEMA — train_service_patterns.csv

  Station Sequence Ordered station stops (→ delimited)

TITLE
  Nigeria Inter-City Travel and Traffic Data

OVERVIEW
  This dataset contains inter-city origin-destination (OD)
  travel records between 9 major Nigerian cities. Each record
  represents a single trip observation with road distance,
  congestion level, average speed, travel time, weather
  condition, number of alternative routes, and a computed
  suitability score.

  The dataset covers 72 unique OD city pairs across
  896 records, with multiple observations per pair under
  varying traffic and weather conditions.

  ⚠ DATA NOTE: Road length values contain high decimal
  precision (e.g. 772.2622439999999 km) and the dataset
  structure suggests this may be simulated or modelled data
  rather than field-collected observations. Users should
  treat values as representative estimates and verify against
  authoritative sources (FMWORKS, FRSC) before citing in
  publications.
